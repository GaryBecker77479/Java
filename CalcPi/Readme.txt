CalcPi

Calculates Pi using 4 different methods.

Method 1:
Cheat
pi ~ 355/113

Method 2:
Gregory-Leibniz Series
pi ~ 4/1 - 4/3 + 4/5 - 4/7 + ...

Method 3:
Nilakantha Series
pi ~ 3.0 + 4/(2*3*4) - 4/(4*5*6) + 4/(6*7*8) - 4/(8*9*10) + ...

Method 4:
Bailey-Borwein-Plouffe Series
pi ~ SUM(k=0 to infinity) ((1/16^k)*(4/(8k+1) - 2/(8k+4) - 1/(8k+5) - 1/(8k+6)))
